Gemini-Powered Magic ✨

✨ Socratic Tutor

Don't just get answers. Gemini adopts a persona (e.g., "Strict Professor") and quizzes you to test deep understanding.

✨ Podcast Scripter

Converts dry lecture notes into an engaging 2-person dialogue script, making study material conversational.

✨ Visual Mnemonics

Generates vivid, creative image descriptions (e.g., "A cell wall made of LEGOs") to help memorize abstract terms.

✨ ELI5 Rewriter

"Explain Like I'm 5": Instantly rewrites complex academic jargon into simple, everyday analogies.
